Individuals behind the workshop

The idea of this workshop stems from a cohort of aspiring academic students from APEC Schools Dasmariñas. In the midst of the global pandemic caused by the COVID-19 outbreak, educational motivation and performance have gradually taken its toll. During that critical period, the students were motivated to initiate a workshop program centered around the establishment of academic resilience. Now, the individuals behind the workshop are riding within the ongoing endeavor of assisting students in their academic journey.
