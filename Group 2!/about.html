Pursuing Academic Excellence

Group 2

Expected Learning Outcomes

In the fullness of time, students that took part in the workshop program are expected to:
- Emulate and become great students in academics with the imparted principle knowledge.
- Adapt to positive academic mentalities and motivations.
- Determine the essential secrets on being a great student that achieves academic excellence.
- Determine the requisite skills to be honed for early craft development and apply them.
- Apply and effect the factors that constitute great students in academics.
- Replace bad study habits with good study habits.
- Eliminate bad academic mentalities and practices, and,
- Describe the relevance of academic discipline, excellence, and proficiency in the real-life.
- Analyze the problems and hindrances against the journey of being great academic students.
